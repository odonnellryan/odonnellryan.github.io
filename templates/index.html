How we work

Our three principle process guides your application development and deployment.

Planning

Plan out your crate to meet your application needs.

Building

Use our tools to build your crate and save it to be deployed anywhere.

Deploying

Deploy your crate with the click of a button.

Using Linux Containers

Linux containers offer many advantages over more traditional development and hosting stacks. Our infrastructure is designed to take advantage of these added benefits.

Allow developers at different product stages to work independently of each other. Quickly deploy new containers at any stage of development.

Use our infrastructure to load-balance and scale. Automatically deploy more crates as needed to keep up with your web traffic.

Keep track of your product with our container history and repository. Quickly revert to a last-known good configuration with zero downtime.
