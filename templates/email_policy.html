Luma Hosting Email Policy

For your ease of use and comfort of mind we've made our email policy incredibly simple.

For anyone who signed up requesting a notification when we go live we will send you one email: to let you know when your account is ready to be set up. After this, we will remove your email address from our systems.

For account holders we will need to keep your email address stored for authentication purposes. However, we respect you, and make the following promises:

Luma Hosting will never email you unless you specifically request us to. This includes:

  • Support Emails
  • Newsletters
  • Promotions

Luma Hosting will never share your email address.

Upon deletion of your account, Luma Hosting will destroy all records of your email address.

We do reserve the right to change this policy, however, we track the time you sign up with us. We'll honor the policy you registered under. We'll make all versions public.

If you have any questions about this policy we're always happy to help answer them. Please contact us, and if you have suggestions know we're listening.
